Foot ball (Soccer)

Football is one of the most popular sports in the world. It is played between two teams, and each team usually has 11 players on the field. The main objective of the game is to score goals by kicking the ball into the opponent’s goal. The game is played on a rectangular field with a goalpost at each end. Players mainly use their feet, head, and chest to control and pass the ball. Only the goalkeeper is allowed to use their hands, and that too only inside the penalty area. A standard football match lasts 90 minutes, divided into two halves of 45 minutes each. The team that scores the most goals by the end of the match wins the game. Football is governed internationally by FIFA, which organizes major tournaments such as the FIFA World Cup. Football helps improve fitness, teamwork, and discipline, which is why it is loved by millions of people around the world.
